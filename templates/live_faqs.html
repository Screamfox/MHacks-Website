FAQ

Where can I park?
We’ll have parking available at 2955 Cass Ave (directly behind the venue).
Can I sleep?
Yes! The Lions, Tigers, and Red Wings Room will be converted to sleeping rooms from 3 - 8 AM Friday night and 12 - 8 AM on Saturday night. We’ll have air mattresses and sleep canoes courtesy of Arbor Networks available.
Where can I shower?
Arbor Networks will be providing shuttles to/from showers at the Boll Family YMCA, as well as providing hygiene products to those that need them. Please contact Arbor Networks if you are interested.
How can I get back to campus during the event?
The Detroit Connector will be stopping at the Detroit Masonic Temple (on Temple St between Cass and 2nd Ave) and can take you back to the Central Campus Transit Center at the following times: You will need a valid MCard to ride the Connector. Please note that we cannot guarantee a seat on the bus, as passenger boarding on a first come/first served basis. Riders should be at the bus stop at least 10 minutes before the scheduled arrival time.
What if I forgot something?
Check out Arbor Network's Lost and Forgotten table to see if they have what you need!
What if I have another question?
Help Desks are located in the Fountain Ballroom and the 2nd Floor. You can also talk to any member of the core team (We’ll be in TEAL t-shirts).
What should I bring?
Bring your laptop, phone, chargers, student ID, toiletries, water bottle, a change of clothes, and a well-rested and open mind. We'll do our best to get you anything else you need.
What can't I bring?
Weapons of any kind, drugs, and alcohol.
